# GyanBrige - Student Guide

## 🎓 How Students Use the Platform

### 1. **Login**
- Open the app at http://localhost:8081
- Use your student credentials:
  - Email: `student@lms.com`
  - Password: `student123`

---

### 2. **Home Page - Browse Videos by Subject**

When you login, you'll see the **Home/Videos** page with:

#### **Subject Filter Tabs** (at the top)
- **All** - Shows all videos
- **Subject tabs** - Each subject created by admin appears as a tab
  - Click any subject tab to filter videos for that subject only
  - Example: Click "Mathematics" to see only math videos

#### **Video List**
Each video card shows:
- **Thumbnail** - Preview image
- **Title** - Video name
- **Subject badge** (blue chip) - Which subject it belongs to
- **Lesson name** - Specific lesson/topic
- **View count** - How many times it's been watched

---

### 3. **Watch a Video**

**To watch:**
1. Click on any video card
2. You'll be taken to the **Video Player** screen

**Video Player Features:**
- ✅ Full HD video playback with HLS streaming
- ✅ **Quality selector** button - Choose quality (Auto/360p/480p/720p/1080p)
- ✅ Standard video controls (play, pause, seek, volume, fullscreen)
- ✅ Automatic quality switching based on your internet speed

---

### 4. **View AI-Generated Notes**

**Notes are displayed below the video** (YouTube-style):

#### **Expandable Notes Section**
- Look for the **"AI-Generated Notes"** section below the video
- It shows a **"Llama 3"** badge (the AI model used)
- Click the section to **expand/collapse** notes

#### **Notes Include:**
- **Summary** - Brief overview of the video content
- **Key Points** - Bullet-pointed important concepts
- **Detailed content** - Comprehensive notes organized by topics
- **Generated date** - When the notes were created

---

### 5. **Navigation**

As a student, you have access to:
- **Videos tab** (Home) - Browse and watch all videos
- **No upload/record tabs** - Students cannot upload videos (only teachers can)

---

## 🔍 Quick Workflow Example

1. **Login** as student
2. **Home page** → See subject tabs (All, Math, Physics, etc.)
3. **Click "Mathematics"** → Only math videos show
4. **Click a video** → Opens video player
5. **Watch video** → Change quality if needed
6. **Scroll down** → Expand "AI-Generated Notes"
7. **Read notes** → See summary and key points
8. **Go back** → Return to browse more videos

## 🎯 Tips for Best Experience

1. **Use subject filters** - Easier to find videos for your current class
2. **Adjust video quality** - Lower quality for slower internet
3. **Read notes while watching** - Better understanding
4. **Notes are searchable** - Use Ctrl+F to find specific topics
